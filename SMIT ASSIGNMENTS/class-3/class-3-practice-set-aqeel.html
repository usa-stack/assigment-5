BOMBAY BIRYANI RECIPE!


Ingredients.

  • For Rice:

    • 2 cups basmati rice
    • 4 cups water
    • 2-3 green cardamoms
    • 4-5 cloves
    • 1 cinnamon stick

  • For biryani:

    • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
    • 2 tbsp butter (optional)
    • 2 large onions (thinly sliced)
    • ½ cup coriander leaves (chopped)
    • 1 tsp biryani masala (optional but adds flavor)
    • Saffron soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk (optional)

    • Instructions:

    1. Marinate the chicken:

      • Let it sit for at least 1 hour (overnight is even better).
      • Mix all the marination ingredients.

    2. Cook The Rice:

      • Wash and soak the basmati rice for 20-30 minutes.
      • Boil water with cardamom, cloves, bay leaf, cinnamon, and salt

    3. Dum (steaming):

      • Cover tightly with a lid or seal with dough.
      • Cook on low flame for 20-25 minutes.
      • (Or bake in the oven at 180°C/350°F for 20 minutes.)

    4. Serve hot with raita, salad, and lemon wedges!
